BETMAN

BETMAN is the core racing execution platform. It is where race-day operators work the card, price the race, track runners, and move from context to action without losing the thread.

  • Race Workspace: open the right meeting and race, keep context coherent, and move directly into analysis.
  • 1-Click Race Analysis: load race profile, sectionals, WHY tags, edge math, and decision context instantly.
  • AI-assisted race analysis: structured racing analysis with stronger guardrails around context, selections, and evidence handling.
  • Market Movers + Interesting Runners: identify price movement and runner-level setup without scanning the whole card manually.
  • Tracked runners: follow active, settled, and history states with fast reopen into race context.

BETMAN Mobile

BETMAN Mobile is the operator-grade companion to the web workspace. It keeps the decision loop live away from desktop so Pulse, tracked runners, market movers, and race reopen workflows stay usable on the move.

  • Pulse on mobile: live feed, history, watchbar focus, and fast open-race actions.
  • Tracked lifecycle: Active, Settled, and History views with cross-surface continuity.
  • Market Movers: dedicated mobile movers screen with country and meeting filtering.
  • Shared control: Pulse thresholds, targeting, and operator settings stay aligned across web and mobile.

BETMAN Pulse

BETMAN Pulse is the premium live signal layer. It sits above generic alerts and is built to surface what matters, in time to act.

  • Pulse Live Feed: premium live signal feed for actionable alerts only.
  • Pulse Control: severity thresholds, tracked-runner override, and meeting / country / race targeting.
  • Pulse History: review what surfaced and refine the signal loop.
  • Premium signal logic: tracked-runner-first prioritisation, pre-jump heat, and jump pulse.
