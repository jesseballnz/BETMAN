# BETMAN Pulse

**BETMAN Pulse** is the premium live signal layer inside BETMAN.

Premium access to Pulse is delivered through the BETMAN Pulse product tier.

Pulse is built for operators who do not want every alert — they want the alerts that matter.

## What Pulse does
Pulse sits above the generic alert layer and focuses operator attention on the signals worth action.

It is designed to:
- prioritise tracked runners
- reduce noise from raw market telemetry
- focus on country / meeting / race targeting
- surface jump-window opportunities
- keep live signal control shared across web and mobile

## Core features
- live Pulse feed
- Pulse history
- Pulse control surface
- tracked-runner-first priority
- severity thresholds
- meeting / country / race targeting
- visible enable state per rule family
- shared web + mobile configuration

## Rule families
- plunges
- drifts
- conflicts
- selection flips
- pre-jump heat
- jump pulse

## How operators use Pulse
1. Set the scope
   - all meetings
   - countries
   - meetings
   - races
   - mixed watchlist

2. Set the gates
   - minimum severity
   - minutes-to-jump window
   - minimum move threshold
   - tracked-runner override

3. Run the live feed
   - monitor live signals
   - reopen exact race context
   - track / untrack directly from the signal flow
   - follow the same runner across web and mobile

4. Review history
   - audit what surfaced
   - confirm whether the feed is catching the right moments
   - refine targeting instead of widening noise

## Why Pulse matters
Pulse is not just another notification surface.
It is the operator attention layer.

The point is not to generate more alerts.
The point is to surface the right signal at the right moment with enough race context to act.

## Live haptics and heat maps
**Live haptics** describes the attention-routing layer that makes the operator feel the right moment to act through:
- jump-window awareness
- tracked-runner prioritisation
- focused live signal design

**Heat Maps** provide a place for thermal intake and horse heat observations so physical-condition signal can sit beside market and simulation signal.

## Positioning
BETMAN Pulse is the premium BETMAN product tier for live signal control.
For launch, access may be broadened operationally, but the product itself is positioned as the premium live signal layer.
