# BETMAN Pulse

New premium product naming:
**BETMAN Pulse. Live Haptics and Heat Maps.**

## Purpose
Pulse is the premium live signal layer inside BETMAN.
It sits above the generic alerts layer and is built to surface the signals worth operator attention.

## Core features
- live Pulse feed
- Pulse history
- Pulse control
- tracked-runner-first priority
- severity thresholds
- meeting / country / race targeting
- visible enable states per rule
- shared web + mobile settings

## Rule families
- plunges
- drifts
- conflicts
- selection flips
- pre-jump heat
- jump pulse

## Live haptics
Live haptics in BETMAN Pulse refers to the attention-routing layer that makes the operator feel the right moment to act through focused live signal design, jump-window awareness, and tracked-runner prioritisation.

## Heat Maps
Heat Maps provide a dedicated location for thermal intake and horse heat observations so physical-condition signal can sit beside market and simulation signal.
